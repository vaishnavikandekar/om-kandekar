Celebrating International Science Day

November 10th

Why Celebrate International Science Day?

International Science Day for Peace and Development, celebrated every year on November 10th, highlights the significant role of science in society and the need to engage the wider public in debates on emerging scientific issues. It also underscores the importance of science for sustainable development and peace.

Key Objectives:

  • To strengthen public awareness of the role of science for peaceful and sustainable societies.
  • To promote national and international solidarity for shared science between countries.
  • To renew national and international commitment for the use of science for the benefit of societies.
  • To draw attention to the challenges faced by science and raise support for the scientific endeavor.

Get Involved!

There are many ways to participate in International Science Day. You can:

  • Attend or organize science-related events, workshops, and lectures.
  • Share interesting science facts and discoveries on social media using relevant hashtags.
  • Engage in discussions about the ethical and societal implications of scientific advancements.
  • Encourage young people to explore the fascinating world of science.
